DO NOT LEAVE LIKE THIS

Our Courtyard Atlanta Northlake hotel in Tucker, GA is conveniently located near wonderful parks, museums and shopping. At our Tucker, GA hotel near Emory University, we feature spacious guests rooms with new luxurious bedding, improved workspace with more lighting and complimentary high-speed Internet.

Begin your day at Marriott Courtyard Atlanta Northlake hotel with breakfast in the Bistro and unwind after meetings or sightseeing in our outdoor seasonal pool. Visit The Market, open 24-Hours offering snacks and beverages. Our hotel in Tucker, GA offers two meeting rooms with on-site catering, ideal for small meetings or social events.

While staying at our Tucker, Georgia hotel, you'll find easy access to all the area attractions including the Georgia Aquarium and Stone Mountain Park. Please note: This hotel does not have an elevator. Start planning your stay today at our Atlanta Midtown hotel and allow us to help you make the most of every travel experience.
